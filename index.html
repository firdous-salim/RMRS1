Academic Programs

Streams & Curriculum

A structured pathway from foundation years to senior secondary specialisation.

01

Primary Wing

Classes 1 – 5 · Foundation years focused on literacy, numeracy & curiosity.

  • Activity-based learning
  • 3 languages introduced
  • Story-time & art
02

Middle School

Classes 6 – 8 · Conceptual depth across science, mathematics & humanities.

  • Project-based modules
  • Robotics & coding intro
  • Inter-house competitions
03

Secondary (9-10)

CBSE Class 9 & 10 · Board preparation with strong fundamentals.

  • Daily revision tests
  • Career counselling
  • Olympiad coaching
04

Senior Secondary

Classes 11 – 12 · Specialisation in Science, Commerce or Humanities.

  • JEE / NEET coaching
  • CUET preparation
  • Industry mentorship
